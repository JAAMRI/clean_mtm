Have Some Questions?

question_answer Can I purchase my meal plan online?

If you live in Ontario, you may be able to purchase your meal plan online through Metro online grocery. Visit www.metro.ca/en/online-grocery to find out if they have pickup or delivery in your area*
If you live in BC, you may be able to purchase your meal plan online through Save on Foods online grocery. Visit https://www.saveonfoods.com/shop-online-how-it-works/# To find out if they have pickup or delivery in your area*
If you live outsite of Ontario or BC, stay tuned! We are always working on improving our service.

*Due to COVID-19 there is higher than normal volume on grocery delivery right now, therefore delivery times may be impacted through Metro & Save on Foods. question_answer How do I build my meal plan?

Easy! From the home page, click 'get started', and make sure you have registered. You’ll then be directed to the ‘Select Recipes’ page. This is where you’ll start browsing through our collection of delicious recipes.
To add a meal to your meal plan, simply click the green ‘+’ button on the top right of the recipe. To see your Meal Plan, click on green next button on the select recipes page.
PS: You can always find these pages on the progress bar or the side menu if you’re on your phone.

 question_answer How do I add a recipe to my favourite list of recipes?

First, click on the little heart icon on the bottom left corner of the recipe image. Second, to see the recipes you’ve previously added to your favourites, click ‘Favourites’ in the side menu on your phone or the navigation bar on desktop.

question_answer How do I see the recipe instructions?

Click on the recipe name or image and the full recipe page will open as a popup. Then you’ll find the cooking instructions in the tabs below the recipe image to the right of the ‘Ingredients’ tab.

question_answer Can I see detailed nutritional information?

Yep! When you click on the recipe name or image, the full recipe page will open as a popup. Then you’ll find nutrition facts in the tabs below the recipe image to the right of the ‘Instructions’ tab.

question_answer Is there an easier way for me to access this website to plan my meals?

Yes! You can add the Meals That Matter website to your phone’s home screen just like an app.
For iOS, launch the Safari browser and go to Mealsthatmatter.com. Tap the Share button, then scroll until you find the ‘Add to Home Screen’ button…and tap to download.
For Android, launch Chrome and go to Mealsthatmatter.com. Tap the menu button, then tap ‘Add to Homescreen’.
>Once downloaded, The Meals That Matter icon will appear on your home screen for you to drag and place wherever you like.

question_answer How much does this cost?

We’re pleased to offer our service free of charge! At Meals That Matter, we have a mission to help Canadians reclaim weeknight cooking, and we hope that our website helps you do just that.
Of course, you’ll still have to pay for your groceries! Those prices and delivery charges (if applicable) are determined by the retailer you choose to shop with.

question_answer Are there Italian/Asian/30 minutes or less recipes?

Yes… and more 😊 The best way to explore these recipes is to use the filter feature on the ‘Select Recipes’ page. We have over 500 recipes that span across multiple recipe categories.
We are always improving and adding to our recipe collection so stay tuned for more dietary options.

question_answer Is membership required in order to create a meal plan/shop?

Yes. In order to create and shop a meal plan you will need to have an account with us. To create a new account simply click the ‘get started’ button on our homepage and then fill out your details to register.
Once registered, you will have access to 500+ recipes with new recipes being added all the time as well as exclusive offers and the ability to simply shop your meal plan online or instore.

question_answer Can I view by category like breakfast, brunch, dinner?

Yes! You can find those categories easily by filtering on the ‘Select Recipes' page.

question_answer My grocery list includes every ingredient in the recipes, including staples I already have like flour, spices, and seasonings. Is there a way to edit my grocery list to remove staples?

If you choose to shop your grocery list online, your items will be sent to the grocery cart on the retailer website. From there, you can remove items you already have before you checkout.
If you choose to shop in-store, you can check off items to indicate that you already have them. Unfortunately you are not able to remove these items completely at this time. However, we’re also looking to improve our service!
