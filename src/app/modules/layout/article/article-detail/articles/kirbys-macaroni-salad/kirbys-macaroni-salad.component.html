“When making my Japanese Macaroni Salad, there are a few important things to remember. 1) Make sure not to skip removing the moisture from the onions and cucumbers. No one likes a wet macaroni salad! 2) The great thing about this recipe is that you can use Hellmann’s Real Mayonnaise, vinegar, and sugar to get that great authentic Japanese flavour in the macaroni salad. 3) Go easy on the Knorr Shichimi Togarashi seasoning at first and add more to taste. If you put too much in the beginning, it’s hard to remove the spiciness!”

Japanese Macaroni Salad Recipe
4 Servings
Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time:10 minutes
113g 8 oz uncooked short-cut macaroni
2 Persian cucumbers, very thinly sliced
1/2 small red onion, very, thinly sliced
5 mL 1 tsp Salt
60 mL 1/2 cup Hellmann's® Real Mayonnaise
2 mL 1/2 tsp. Knorr® Shichimi Togarashi Seasoning Blend or more to taste
5 mL 1 tsp. rice wine vinegar
2mL 1/2 tsp granulated sugar
4 slices ham, cut into strips
60mL 1/2 cup drained canned corn
2 hard cooked egg, chopped
1
Cook macaroni according to package directions. Drain, rinse under cold water, then drain again.
2
Meanwhile, combine cucumbers, onion and salt in medium bowl. Lightly massage the salt into the cucumber and onion. (This will help remove the moisture.) Let stand 5 minutes. Squeeze water from cucumber and onion, then rinse under cold water.
3
Combine Hellmann's® Real Mayonnaise and Knorr® Shichimi Togarashi Seasoning Blend, vinegar and sugar in large bowl. Add macaroni, cucumber-onion mixture, ham and corn and toss to mix and coat. Gently stir in egg. Serve, or cover and refrigerate until cold.
TIP:
If desired, adjust salad to taste, adding more Knorr® Shichimi Togarashi Seasoning Blend if you want it more spicy, more Hellmann's® Real Mayonnaise if you want it more creamy.
