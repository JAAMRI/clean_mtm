Middle Eastern Neatball Wrap Recipe
4 Servings
Prep Time: 25 minutes Cook Time:15 minutes
450g 1bl. ground chicken
1 zucchini, grated
250mL 1 cup breadcrumbs, divided
15 mL 1Tbsp Knorr® Zaatar Seasoning Blend
2 cloves garlic, minced
5 mL 1 tsp. ground cumin
5 mL 1 tsp. ground coriander
30 mL 2 tbsp extra viring olive oil
1 lemon
4 pita breads or wraps
30 mL 2 Tbsp. Hellmann's® Organic™ Spicy Chipotle Mayonnaise
75 mL 1/3 cup chopped mint leaves
30 mL 3 Tbsp. pomegranate molasses
1
Preheat the oven to 450°F (230° C). Line rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper or greased foil.
2
Combine chicken, grated zucchini, 1/2 cup (125 mL) breadcrumbs, Knorr® Harissa Seasoning Blend, garlic, cumin and coriander in medium bowl. Coat hands with olive oil and roll 1 Tbsp. (15 mL) mixture into balls, then roll into remaining breadcrumbs.
3
Arrange on prepared baking sheet. Arrange lemon slices around meatballs. Bake 15 minutes or until golden brown and thoroughly cooked.
4
Spread 1/2 Tbsp. (7 mL) Hellmann's® Organic™ Spicy Chipotle Mayonnaise onto each pita. Add meatballs and mint leaves and lettuce and cucumbers, if desired, then drizzle with pomegranate molasses.
TIP:
Want to skip the bread? Roll meatballs up in lettuce leaves instead.
